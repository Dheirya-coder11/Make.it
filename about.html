About Us

Welcome to Navi Mumbai 3142

In the year 3142, Navi Mumbai stands as a beacon of advanced technology and innovation. Our mission is to take you on a journey through this futuristic cityscape, exploring its towering skyscrapers, neon-lit streets, and the digital marvels that define its unique charm.

Our Vision

We envision a future where technology and humanity coexist in harmony. Through our website, we aim to shed light on the incredible advancements and the challenges that come with them. Central to our narrative is the battle against cyberpsychosis, a condition triggered by excessive cybernetic augmentation.

Our Story

At the heart of our story is Pramesh Chitrakar, known as 'ded_l0ck'. As a skilled hacker and cybernetically enhanced vigilante, Pramesh navigates the complex urban landscape to uncover the truth behind a series of cyberpsychosis incidents. His journey is one of courage, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of justice.

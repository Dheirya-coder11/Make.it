ded_l0ck's Journey

Welcome to the journey of Pramesh Chitrakar, known as 'ded_l0ck'. In the year 3142, amidst the advanced cityscape of Navi Mumbai, he fights against the rising threat of cyberpsychosis. Scroll down to explore his story, from humble beginnings to heroic feats, and discover how one man stands as the last hope for a city's future.

The Beginning

Pramesh Chitrakar grew up in the bustling metropolis of Navi Mumbai, fascinated by technology and hacking from an early age. Raised by a single mother who worked tirelessly to support them, Pramesh found solace in the digital world. His natural talent for hacking was both a hobby and a means of coping with the challenges of city life. Little did he know, this skill would become crucial in his fight against a looming threat.

First Encouter

Pramesh’s life changed when he first witnessed the horrors of cyberpsychosis. A close friend, overwhelmed by cybernetic enhancements, lost touch with reality, becoming a danger to everyone around him. Determined to prevent others from suffering the same fate, Pramesh vowed to uncover the truth behind cyberpsychosis and protect his community

Rising Threat

As cybernetic augmentation became more prevalent, so did the cases of cyberpsychosis. Navi Mumbai’s hospitals were overwhelmed with patients exhibiting violent and unpredictable behavior. The government, initially dismissive, was forced to acknowledge the crisis as incidents escalated, threatening the city’s stability.

Allies and Enemies

Pramesh found allies in unexpected places. Dr. Amir Khan, a neuroscientist, provided invaluable insights into the neurological effects of cyberpsychosis. Raj, a former gang member turned tech expert, offered street-level intelligence and technical support. Together, they formed a formidable team against the cybernetic menace.


Standing in their way were powerful adversaries. Corporate tycoons profiting from the sale of faulty cybernetic implants, and corrupt officials willing to turn a blind eye for the right price. These enemies had resources and influence, making Pramesh’s mission even more perilous.

Climactic Confrontation

The final battle saw Pramesh confronting the mastermind behind the cyberpsychosis outbreak. A climactic showdown in the heart of Navi Mumbai’s tech district ensued, filled with high-stakes hacking and intense combat. Pramesh’s determination and skill were put to the ultimate test as he fought to save his city.


Emerging victorious, Pramesh exposed the truth, leading to the arrest of those responsible and a citywide effort to address cyberpsychosis.
